Daft Punk

Greatest Hits

One More Time

One of Daft Punk's most recognizable tracks, and one of my favorite electronic tracks with it's infectious melody. Listening to this makes you want to get up and dance! This house-driven melody has become a staple in clubs worldwide.

Around the World

This song features an energetic and unique music video with repetitive choreography that complements the hypnotic rhythm of the song. The song’s funky bassline gives it a futuristic and almost robotic quality, reflecting Daft Punk’s signature style.


Daft Punk (2021). Daft Punk - Around The World (Official Music Video Remastered). [online video].
Available at: YouTube. Accessed: 9 March 2025

Lose Yourself to Dance

Featuring a funky groove, this song is a fantastic collaboration with Pharrell Williams. Love how the video is shot in a circular motion around one small stage of irridescent outfits and instruments. Losing yourself to dance is the best way to enjoy some good music.


Daft Punk (2013). Daft Punk - Lose Yourself to Dance (Official Version). [online video].
Available at: YouTube. Accessed: 9 March 2025
